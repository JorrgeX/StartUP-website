Who we are...

StartUP plans to integrate artificial intelligence into a business platform by training algorithms through machine learning, powered by the cloud, to recognize and adapt to the needs of minority business owners with lower levels of income and education.

What we do...

Most startups fail. In fact, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, only 25% of startups survive long enough to see their 15th year of operations. Why do startups fail in the first place? If we look at study results published by market research companies such as CB Insights, we can see that the number one reason for startup failure is "no market need" with "running out of cash" coming in second. This speaks volumes about how uninformed business owners are about the market they are entering. The bottom line isn't surprising. After all, most entrepreneurs are average adults who have full-time jobs and consequently, very limited time and capital. This problem only gets worse when we take a look at minorities in terms of entrepreneurship. Statistically, lower levels of income and education mean that it is generally harder for minorities to start successful businesses due to larger barriers of entry. StartUP aims to change all this and empower the entrepreneur with an A.I. powered application that provides data and resources to business owners all in one place, while simultaneously "holding the hand" of newer entrepreneurs as they setup their business. StartUP plans to integrate artificial intelligence into the platform by training algorithms through machine learning, powered by the cloud, to recognize and adapt to the needs of business owners. The secondary goal of StartUP is to level the playing field for minorities by providing ease of access to information in easy-to-consume portions. We see the future of StartUP as one in which the artificial intelligence on the platform can be utilized to connect entrepreneurs of similar niches together in order to encourage market collaboration while maintaining the competition.
